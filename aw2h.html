Frequently Asked Questions for AW2H Locks:

L1) How to Reprogram a lock as a guest or common door?



Large Keypad w/ Slider (StandAlone Unit) Instrctions

You'll have to Detach the Keypad from Slider to be able to use it for programming locks
*Check Date & Time First:
Sign in w/ Staff Code and Password, Press F1(Ok)
Press 7 – DTU Setting Menu
Press 1 – Date and Time settings
Put in the correct Date and Time (MILITARY TIME)


Programming a new guest door lock.
a) Turn off FDS.
b) Remove the Data Transfer Unit from the base unit.
c) xxx
d) Turn on the DTU.
e) Enter your staff code and password, then press F1
f) Go to 1. for Normal Mode.
g) Go to 4. for Data Mode.
h) Room Type is 02 for guest and press F1.
i) Input the room number and press enter.
j) Insert any card in lock (red light is ok), Aim unit at lock and press SEND/RCV.
k) (If you have a Brand new reader, you have to sign in with the maintenance code. It is normal to get a Hotel Code Error, Press the Error Reset Button
l) Press F1 for Retry Download.
m) DTU will say “Now Loading” and “Loading End” when finished
*Aim at the center of the black stripe (3-4 inches away)
*The Item Resel. Button is your back button if you need to go to a previous choice


Programming a new lock as a common door.
a) Follow steps a-g from above.
b) Room type is 01 common and press enter.
c) Press F1.
d) Press F4 to edit, (then up or down arrows to navigate)
e) If you to allow guests to access the door 7 days a week, then all Valid Days should be highlighted (dark square)
f) Press the down arrow a few times to get to Valid Time. Enter the (MILITARY) time you want to allow guests to be able to access the door (as long as they have a card)
**If you want only staff to be able to access it, enter the Valid Time as 00:00-00:01
Then Press F1 to save
g) (Optional step, skip if not needed) ** If you want the door to say open and Not require keys during a certain time- press the down arrow a few times until you see Auto TZ1, then Press F4, press enter on OFF and change it to ON. Then press the down arrow and enter the Start and End times of the door staying open (Military Time), Press F1 to save.
h) (Very Important Step) Press down arrow till you get to Room Range 1
i) Press F4 to edit.
j) Input lowest room in range and press enter.
k) Input highest room in range and press F1 for ok. v l) Press F1 for ok (again)
m) Insert any card in lock (red light is ok), Aim unit at lock and press SEND/RCV.
n) (If you have a Brand new reader, you have to sign in with the maintenance code. It is normal to get a Hotel Code Error, Press the Error Reset Button
o) Press F1 to Retry Download.
p) Aim DTU at lock and press the SEND/RCV key.
q) DTU will say “Now Loading” and “Loading End” when finished.
*Aim at the center of the black stripe (3-4 inches away)
*The Item Resel. Button is your back button if you need to go to a previous choice

L2) Lock lights up red and doesn't allow access to the room.

-Reprogram the lock using your DTU lock programmer (there's 2 kinds, choose the one you have), see Question L1 above

See question L6 (Clearing the sequence) below

-For guest key still lighting up red, try making a guest key with the check time of 1am of the current day, and check out of 1 week from from today, then using it on the lock
If you get it to work it may be a date/time issue.
-For staff keys not working, try clearing the sequence (Question L6 below), then using a card again
or make a New Issue under submaster card and try the card

-If it is still not working with any card, you may have to replace the card reader.


L3A) Lock is out of power and unresponsive to any card

OR

Plug Emergency Battery kit undernearth lock and use key card to gain entry
or
Use the Physical Override Key if your lock has that feature

Replace the lock's batteries once room is accessed, and Reprogram the lock to correct date/time.
Try any card after and see if it lights up green.

If it is still unresponsive after replacing the batteries, try exchanging it's back portion (which has the battery holder with a different lock
If it works after the exchange, then you'll need a new battery holder
If it's still unresponsive after exchanging the battery holder, you'll have to replace the card reader inside.
You can call our office if you need to order another one.

Instructions for MSR SLIDER & ATHENA SMART CARD READER- Keymaker is a Windows 10 PC (new type)-

Open the AL5H PC Server Program

b) Click maintenance
c) Click hotel setup
d) Click submaster group
e) Click add
f) Enter a name (example: manager/front desk/housekeeperr)

h) Check the valid date, this shows how long cards make with this account will work.
i) Change valid time ( if you want their cards to work 24/7 then change it to 00;00 to 23:59)
j) Click add (f1) on lower left
k) Command should say room range, enter the lowest and the highest room in property then press ok (f9)

Issuing Staff keys with the AL5H PC Server
Open the AL5H PC Server Program
Sign in with Manager's Staff Code and Password
Click Management, Click Issue Submaster Card
Choose an account on Submaster Group, check the valid date and time to make sure it will work
On the command section, check to see if it has a room range (those are the rooms the account is allowed to access
You'll have two choices, either New or Duplicate
Choose New if you want the card to disable any previous cards under the same account (when used on a lock)
or
Choose Duplicate if you Do Not want to disable any previous cards made under the same account (when used on a lock)
Click issue and Insert a white, Chip Card on the Athena Smart Card Writer

Understanding Sequences:
Whenver you make a card or batch of cards, a sequence number is assigned to them.
When you make a New Issue on the same guest room, or staff member's account, a higher level sequence number is assigned to it
When you use the newer card on a lock, it will no longer accept any lower sequence or older cards, thus the sequence error
To avoid sequence error, you make a duplicate card in order not to assign a higher sequence number and disable the previous card
Also note that batches of cards, i.e. 10 cards made as a set have identical sequence numbers and will not cancel each other out


L7) Disabling Lost/Stolen Staff or Guest Keys

Create a new issue of the staff or guest key and run it by the appropriate door.

The old keys will no longer work on a lock that is activated by a new issue of keys.
